DeepESDL

Welcome to the online documentation of DeepESDL – ESA’s Deep Earth System Data Laboratory, a platform providing analysis-ready data cube in a powerful, virtual laboratory to the Earth Science research community. DeepESDL offers a full suite of services to facilitate data exploitation, share data and source code, and publish results. Special emphasize is put on improving the support for machine learning and artificial intelligence approaches, which includes the preparation of AI-ready datasets, providing a programming environment with relevant libraries and packages, and the resources to execute processing pipelines. For more information and access to the lab please visit the DeepESDL website .
